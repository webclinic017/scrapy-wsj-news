Mr. Macron easily won a second mandate in April, but on Sunday his allies lost control of the National Assembly. His centrist Ensemble block won 245 seats, well short of the 289 needed for an absolute majority. It’s a significant fall from the 350 Mr. Macron’s group won in 2017.

Some executives’ compensation is better aligned with shareholder interests than others. While most pay packages are bolstered by restricted stock units that might never pay off, several handsomely paid executives last year helmed newly public companies that saw their stocks pop and quickly flop.
